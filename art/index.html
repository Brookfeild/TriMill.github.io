Art

These programs create generative art. Most of them use the Javascript library p5.js, which you can download here [→].

List of art:

  • Square Subdivision - Click to split the squares into smaller squares
  • Circle Packing - Circles filling in the spaces between other circles
  • Voronoi - Creates Voronoi diagrams very slowly
  • Line Patterns - Lines connecting points that make neat patterns
